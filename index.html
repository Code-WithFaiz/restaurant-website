Our Story

Slow food, warm light.

Ember & Saffron began with a simple idea: that a meal is not just eaten, it's lived in. Every table is lit by candle, every spice is ground the day it's used, and every dish arrives when it's ready — not a minute before.

Our kitchen draws from generations of Indian cooking, reworked for a quieter, slower kind of evening.
